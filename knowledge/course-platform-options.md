## My Recommendation: Thinkific or Teachable

### Thinkific (Best for Starting)
- **Free until you sell** — No monthly fee until you have students
- **Clean interface** — Easy to upload PDFs, videos
- **Email capture** — Built-in lead forms
- **Pay wall** — Course pricing built-in
- **Straightforward** — No bloat

### Teachable (Best for Growth)
- **$39/mo** (after free tier)
- **More features** — Email marketing, analytics
- **Payment processing** — Built in
- **Good support** — Easier if you need help

## Quick Start Recommendation

**Start with Thinkific:**
1. Go to thinkific.com → Sign up (free)
2. Create your "course" (Operations Guide)
3. Upload the PDF chapters
4. Set pricing ($67 for Tier 2)
5. Connect Stripe or PayPal
6. Share the link

Done. You have a paid course with email capture.
