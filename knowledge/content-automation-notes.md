# Content Automation Notes

## Date: April 6, 2026

---

## Goal
Automate content creation to minimize Tim's time while building social media following.

## Constraints
- Tim wants minimal time commitment
- Wants to automate as much as possible
- No camera required
- Equipment: Has camera/lighting, uses Canva for editing

---

## Options Explored

### 1. HeyGen AI Avatar
- Pros: No filming, professional look
- Cons: Tim found it "bogging down," costs $30/mo, requires script generation

### 2. Canva AI Voice + CapCut (Selected)
- Workflow: Write script → Canva AI voice reads it → CapCut auto-captions/edits
- Estimated time: ~4 min/video
- Tools needed: Canva Pro ($15/mo), CapCut (free)

### 3. Pictory.ai
- Turns article/URL into narrated video
- $19/mo, alternative to Canva

### 4. Hire VA
- $15-30/hr to generate HeyGen videos

---

## Final Workflow Decision

Tim will use:
1. **Scripts** - I provide (ready to record)
2. **Canva** - AI voice reads script (no mic needed)
3. **CapCut** - For captions (manual method confirmed working)
4. **Meta Business Suite** - Schedule posts
