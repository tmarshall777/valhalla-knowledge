# Chapter 8: Communication & Marketing

## The Communication Rule

**Tell them what they need to know BEFORE they need to know it.**

Parents will never complain "you're telling me too much." They complain when they don't know what's happening.

This chapter covers every communication touchpoint — from sign-up to post-event.

---

## The Communication Timeline

### 8 Weeks Out: Initial Announcement

**What's sent:** Registration announcement
**Channel:** Email to list + social media
**Content:**
- Event date
- Venue
- Registration link
- Early bird deadline
- Highlights (what makes your event special)

### 6 Weeks Out: Push Campaign

**What's sent:** Email + social posts
**Content:**
- Registration still open
- Highlight differentiators
- Limited early bird
- Share unique features (on-time, awards, etc.)

### 4 Weeks Out: Final Push

**What's sent:** Email + paid social
**Content:**
- Early bird ending soon
- Last chance for discount
- Final push to register

### 2 Weeks Out: Pre-Event Details

**What's sent:** Detailed email + website update
**Content:**
- Full schedule
- Weigh-in options
- Start times by division
- Parking info
- What to bring

### 1 Week Out: Final Reminders

**What's sent:** Email + social
**Content:**
- Day-of schedule
- Weigh-in reminder
- Parking reminder
- Emergency contact info

### Day Before: Confirmation

**What's sent:** Quick email/Text
**Content:**
- Venue address
- Final schedule
- Good luck message

## Pre-Event Email Templates

### Template 1: Registration Announcement

> Subject: [Event Name] — Registration Now Open!
>
> Hey families!
>
> We're excited to announce registration is open for [Event Name]!
>
> 📅 Date: [Date]
> 📍 Venue: [Venue Name & Address]
> 🏆 What to expect: On-time start, professional refs, custom awards
>
> Register now to secure your spot:
> [Link]
>
> Early bird pricing: [Price] (ends [Date])
>
> See you on the mat!
> — Tim

### Template 2: Pre-Event Details

> Subject: Everything You Need for [Event Name]
>
> Parents & Coaches,
>
> Here are all the details for this weekend's event:
>
> **Schedule:**
> - Morning Session (6U, 10U): 9:00 AM start
> - Noon Session (8U, 12U): 12:00 PM start
> - Evening Session (15U): 3:00 PM start
>
> **Weigh-ins:**
> - [Option 1]: Virtual weigh-in (Sat morning)
> - [Option 2]: Saturday 7-8 PM at venue
> - [Option 3]: Day-of (see website for times)
>
> **Parking:** Free at [address]. Overflow at [address].
>
> **What to bring:** Singlet, shoes, water, money for concessions
>
> Full details: [Website link]
>
> Questions? Reply to this email.
> — Tim

### Template 3: Day-of Reminder

> Subject: See You Tomorrow! (Quick Reminders)
>
> Quick reminders for tomorrow:
>
> 🕗 Doors open at [Time]
> ⚖️ Weigh-ins open at [Time]
> 🏟️ Venue: [Name & Address]
> 🅿️ Parking: [Instructions]
>
> Download the schedule here: [Link]
>
> Good luck to all the wrestlers!
> — Tim

## Communication Channels

### Email (Primary)
- Use Mailchimp, ConvertKit, or Beehiiv
- Segment by division
- Track open rates
- A/B test subject lines

### Social Media (Secondary)
- Facebook: Events + posts
- Instagram: Reels + stories
- TikTok: Short videos

### Website
- Central hub for all info
- Update frequently
- FAQ section

### Text/Notifications (Urgent Only)
- For last-minute changes
- Use TrackWrestling notifications
- Or software like TextNow

## Content That Converts

### What to Highlight

| For Parents | For Coaches |
|-------------|-------------|
| Start on time | Quality competition |
| Short sessions | Accurate seeding |
| Custom awards | Enough matches |
| Easy parking | Professional setup |
| Family-friendly | Good communication |

### Key Messages to Repeat

1. **We start on time**
2. **We end in 3 hours**
3. **No membership needed**
4. **Quality refs**
5. **Custom awards**
