# Chapter 15: 48-Hour Follow Up

## The Critical Window

The 48 hours after your event are crucial for building repeat customers. Here's how to capitalize.

---

## Thank You Email (Within 24 Hours)

### Template

> Subject: Thank You from [Event Name]!
>
> Hey Families!
>
> THANK YOU for joining us at [Event Name]!
>
> We hope you had a great experience. Our goal is always to provide the best tournament experience possible — and we're glad you were part of it.
>
> Results are live on our website: [Link]
>
> Our next event is [Date]! Registration opens [Date]. Don't miss it!
>
> See you on the mat!
> — Tim

---

## Social Media Posts (Within 48 Hours)

### Post-Event Content

- Photo gallery from awards
- "Thanks for coming!" message
- Highlights from the event
- Next event announcement

### Example Post

> "What an amazing day at [Event Name]! 300+ wrestlers, incredible competition, and the best fans in youth wrestling. Thank you to everyone who came out!
> 
> Missed us? Our next event is [Date] — registration opens soon!
> 
> #YouthWrestling #[EventName]"

## Collecting Feedback

### Survey to Distribute

Send to parents 48 hours post-event:

**Questions:**
1. How likely are you to return to our event? (1-10)
2. What did we do well?
3. What could we improve?
4. Would you recommend us to a friend?

### Feedback Review

- Compile all responses
- Look for patterns
- Note what to change
- Thank those who give detailed feedback
