## Platform Options

| Platform | Pros | Cons | Cost |
|----------|------|------|------|
| **Teachable** | Easy setup, handles payments, student portal | Takes revenue share | Free tier / $39/mo |
| **Kajabi** | All-in-one, email built-in, beautiful | Expensive | $149-399/mo |
| **Thinkific** | Free until you sell, good features | Less marketing tools | Free / $49/mo |
| **Gumroad** | Simple, instant pay, easy for digital products | Less course-focused | 10% fee |
| **Own Website + Stripe** | Full control, no monthly fees | More tech setup | Hosting + Stripe fees |

**Recommendation**: Start with **Teachable** or **Thinkific** (free to launch, minimal upfront)

## Marketing Strategy (National Reach)

### 1. Content Marketing (Attract)
- YouTube videos on TrackWrestling tips
- Blog posts: "10 Mistakes New Tournament Directors Make"
- TikTok/IG: Short tips, behind-the-scenes

### 2. Email List Building (Nurture)
- Free lead magnet → email sequence
- 5-email automated sequence:
  1. Welcome + introduce story
  2. Problem (running tournaments is hard)
  3. Solution (here's what works)
  4. Product preview
  5. Call to action

### 3. Partnerships (Distribute)
- Reach out to state wrestling associations
- Partner with club networks
- Affiliate with gear brands (promote their products, they promote your courses)

### 4. Webinars (Convert)
- Free "TrackWrestling Q&A" webinar
- Pitch mid-tier course at end

### 5. Social Proof
- Get first 10 customers free or discounted
- Testimonials → sales page
- Case studies
