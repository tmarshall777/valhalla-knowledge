## Step 2: Create Voiceover in Canva

1. **Open Canva** → Click "Create a design" → Choose "Video" (any size, 9:16 works best for social)

2. **Add your script**:
   - Click "Text" on left sidebar
   - Paste script into the text box
   - Resize/move text where you want it (usually center)

3. **Generate AI Voice**:
   - Click "Elements" → search "voiceover" OR
   - Click "Apps" in left sidebar → search "Text to Speech" or "Voiceover"
   - Paste script into the tool
   - Select voice (recommended: "American Male - Ryan" or similar clear voice)
   - Click "Generate"
   - It will add audio to your timeline

4. **Add Background** (optional):
   - Click "Elements" → Uploads → drop in your B-roll footage
   - Video will play behind your text

5. **Export**:
   - Click "Share" (top right) → "Download" → "MP4 Video"
   - Save to your device

**Time: ~2 minutes**

---

## Step 3: Auto-Edit in CapCut

1. **Open CapCut** → Tap "+" → select your Canva export

2. **Auto-Edit (AI Smart Cut)**:
   - After importing, look for "Auto Cut" or "AI Edit" or "One-Click Edit"
   - Or: Scroll to "Smart Cut" features
   - Tap it — CapCut will:
     - Add captions automatically
     - Crop to vertical (9:16)
     - Add transitions between clips
     - Beat-sync to audio

3. **Optional Manual Tweaks**:
   - Edit text if caption is wrong
   - Adjust timing if needed
   - Add effects/stickers from sidebar

4. **Export**:
   - Tap "Export" (bottom right) → "1080p" or "4K" → Save to device

**Time: ~2 minutes**

## Pro Tips

- **Batch everything**: Do all 5 scripts in Canva, then all 5 in CapCut
- **Save templates**: In Canva, save your branded template (fonts, colors, logo) so every video looks consistent
- **Hashtag set**: Save this set for every post:
  - #wrestling #youthwrestling #wrestlingtournament #valhallawrestling #parents #coaches
- **Captions**: CapCut auto-adds, but always check — AI isn't perfect
- **Voice selection**: Test a few voices in Canva first to find your favorite

## Troubleshooting

**Canva voiceover not working?**
- Make sure you're logged into Canva Pro
- Try a different voice from the list

**CapCut auto-edit not available?**
- Use the "Captions" feature manually: tap "Captions" → "Auto-Generate"
- Then manually trim/split clips

**Video too long?**
- CapCut has "Speed" — increase to 1.5x if needed
- Or trim in the timeline
