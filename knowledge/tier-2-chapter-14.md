# Chapter 14: Immediate Post-Event

## Right After the Last Match

The event ends, but your work isn't done. Here's what to do immediately after.

---

## The Closing Hour

### Final Matches Complete
- Award ceremony
- Final announcements
- Thank you to staff
- Next event announcement

### Immediately After
1. **Finalize results**
   - Enter all results in TrackWrestling
   - Verify complete
   - Publish live results

2. **Staff payment**
   - Pay referees
   - Pay table workers
   - Thank them personally
   - Schedule next event for reliable staff

3. **Venue teardown**
   - Collect all equipment
   - Clean up venue
   - Return venue to normal configuration
   - Check for lost items

4. **Staff debrief (quick)**
   - What went well?
   - What needs improvement?
   - Thank everyone

---

## Checklist: Post-Event (Same Day)

- [ ] All results entered in TrackWrestling
- [ ] Results published online
- [ ] Staff paid
- [ ] Equipment packed
- [ ] Venue cleaned
- [ ] Venue inspected
- [ ] Any lost items collected
- [ ] Security deposit (if applicable)
