# Chapter 11: Event Day — Morning Operations

## The Big Day

Event day. This is where all your preparation either works — or doesn't. Let's make sure it works.

---

## Morning Timeline

### 5:00 AM — Wake Up
- Have coffee
- Review final checklist
- Don't panic — trust your prep

### 7:00 AM — Arrive at Venue
- Check venue (is everything as you left it?)
- Verify power on
- Turn on laptops
- Test WiFi connection

### 7:30 AM — Staff Arrives
- Staff check-in
- Distribute credentials/wristbands
- Staff meeting (15 minutes)

### 8:00 AM — Staff Meeting Agenda
1. Welcome + thank you
2. Review schedule
3. Assign positions
4. Break schedule
5. Emergency procedures
6. Who to contact (floor manager)
7. "Let's have a great event!"

### 8:30 AM — Final Setup
- Ensure mats ready
- Verify scoreboard working
- Check registration area
- Final communication if needed

### 9:00 AM — WEIGH-INS CLOSE
- Ensure all wrestlers weighed in
- Close weigh-in station
- Deadline for scratches

### 9:15 AM — BRACKETS FINALIZED
- NO more adjustments
- Final brackets locked
- Print final (if needed)

### 9:30 AM — FIRST CALL
- Announce first matches
- Get wrestlers to staging area

### 10:00 AM — WRESTLING BEGINS
- First match on ALL mats
- Floor manager monitors
- Go!

---

## Hour-by-Hour Operations

### First Hour (9-10 AM)

**TD Responsibilities:**
- Monitor for any issues
- Handle disputes through floor manager
- Watch weigh-in closeout

**Staff:**
- Refs ready at mats
- Table workers at stations
- Runners in staging area

### Second Hour (10-11 AM)

- Mat 1 through Mat N all running
- Floor manager handling issues
- TD monitors flow

### Third Hour (11 AM - 12 PM)
- First session should be wrapping up
- Awards ceremony begins
- Second session getting ready

---

## Staff Positions During Event

### Floor Manager
- Located at center table/head table
- Has final say on disputes
- Coordinates all mats
- Has access to TD for escalation

### Head Table
- Coordinates table workers
- Handles bracket corrections
- Has all bracket sheets
- Communication hub

### Runners
- Know exactly where staging area is
- Know how to call wrestlers
- Know where each mat is
- Have walkie talkies
